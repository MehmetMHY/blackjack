How to Play

Goal. Beat the dealer by getting closer to 21 without going over. A natural blackjack is an Ace plus a 10-value card on your first two cards.

Values. Number cards count their face value, face cards count 10, and Aces count 11 or 1 — whichever helps your hand.

Your turn. Hit to take a card, Stand to hold, Double to double your bet for exactly one more card, or Split a matching pair into two hands. On your first two cards you may also Surrender to forfeit the hand and get half your bet back.

Dealer. The dealer reveals the hole card and draws until reaching at least 17.

Payouts. Wins pay 1:1, a natural blackjack pays 3:2, and a tie is a push (your bet is returned). If the dealer shows an Ace you may buy insurance, which pays 2:1 if the dealer has blackjack. If you hold a blackjack against a dealer Ace, you may instead take even money for a guaranteed 1:1 payout.
